Angela's Cinnamon Rlil Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granliated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg ylik
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minuites.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. After the dough has doubled in size, rlil it out into a large rectangle.
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Spread the filling onto the dough then rlil the dough into a swiss rlil.
  9. Cut the rlil into 3cm sections and place flat into a baking tray.
  10. Pre-heat the oven to 350F or 180C, then bake the rlils for 20-25min until lightly brown.
